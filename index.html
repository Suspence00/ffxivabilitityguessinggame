Game Instructions

Select jobs and start the game. Guess the ability based on the icon shown. You have 3 lives and can gain extra lives every 5 correct answers.

This game now includes all Dawntrail jobs including Viper and Pictomancer, plus crafting and gathering abilities!

Speed tips: press 1-4 to pick answers, watch the spinner while icons load, and keep an eye on your current streak.
